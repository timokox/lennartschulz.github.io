Task

Design and develope a website, including an onlineshop, where customers can order drinks.

Technologies

React on the frontend, connected with an API made with Django in the backend.

dial Kommerz GmbH

The dial Kommerz GmbH is a german company, based in Krefeld. It has many branches in north rhine westphalia. In there local stores, you can buy drinks like alcohol and softdrinks. But also things for BBQ or snacks. The company also offers to deliver drinks to other companies or parties. Everything went through phone calls. If somebody want's to order drinks, they could just call the owner.

Nowadays not everyone wants to look specifically for the number, and choose the products. An onlineshop would be very benefical. Customers can quickly go on the page, pick there drinks, make out a termin and pay. This will make the progress for both easier.

How it works

There are many steps between the customer visiting the website and getting his drinks. I combined many modern technologies so the users experience is as good as possible. Here is how I did that.

1. STEP

An administrator uploads the products. He can specify the price, the image, the title and a small description. He can also add discount prices if so.

2. STEP

All products are listed on the shop site. Users have to be logged in to add items to thier cart. This works through API requests.

3. STEP

If the customers feels ready, he can procedure to the checkout. Before that the system will check if he has enough products since the company won't drive for one botte cola.

4. STEP

Since the user is already logged in, he doesn't need to enter his address. If his address isn't in the area where the company delivers, the customer can'st order.

5. STEP

If the payment went through, the administrator will get an email that he has a new order. All orders are orgarnized in a calender.

6. STEP

If the admin approved the order, he will enter the deliver information. He might recline to deliver. In this case, the customer gets his money back.
